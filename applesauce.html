applesauce

Applesauce is whimsical story that follows a freshly ripened Apple and his cynical observer Sonny as he sets out in search of adventure and encounters his first human.

This two-minute 3D animation was created on a team of six animators and developers over a 10-week period, during which we crafted each character and scene from scratch under a typical animation pipeline workflow. My individual roles included background painting, facial and speech animation, compositing, and post-production editing.
